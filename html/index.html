HACK-A-TON

"A MINI HACKATHON BY US,FOR US"

WHAT IS HACK-A-TON?

Hack-a-ton is a friendly hackathon among friends aimed at improving coding skills and fostering collaboration. Participants challenge each other to build projects, solve problems, and learn new technologies in a supportive environment, enhancing both individual abilities and teamwork. It's a fun and productive way to grow together.

WHY WAS IT NEEDED?

Hack-a-ton was needed to create a supportive space for friends to enhance their coding skills and collaborate on projects. It addresses the gap in practical, hands-on learning and teamwork often missing in traditional education. By working together, participants can share knowledge, tackle challenges, and motivate each other to improve.

HOW IS HACK-A-TON CONDUCTED?

Hack-a-ton is conducted on Discord, moderated by Anish, and spans a week. Participants join dedicated channels for brainstorming, coding, and troubleshooting. Daily check-ins and collaborative sessions are scheduled to ensure progress and engagement. Anish facilitates discussions, provides guidance, and helps resolve any issues, ensuring a smooth and productive experience.

PREP-A-TON

HOW IS PREP-A-TON BENIFICIAL

Prep-a-ton was beneficial because it allowed participants to individually enhance their skills, familiarize themselves with necessary tools, and build confidence before the main hackathon. By creating a website, they gained practical experience and were better prepared for collaborative challenges, ensuring a smoother and more productive Hack-a-ton.
